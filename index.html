About Keri Crafts

A brand rooted in craft.

Keri Crafts is built around thoughtful Gujarati offerings that feel rooted, considered, and personal rather than mass made.

The brand draws from Gujarati flavour memory and a way of making that values care, patience, and detail. Instead of endless variety or continuous stock, Keri Crafts focuses on smaller batches that feel more intentional, more special, and closer to the maker.

Everything about the brand is designed to feel warm, direct, and thoughtfully presented — from the offerings themselves to the way they are shared and ordered.

The Craft Behind the Brand

Made with intention, not volume.

Keri Crafts is shaped by a simple idea: food can feel more meaningful when it is made with care, shared thoughtfully, and presented beautifully.

Rather than building around scale, the brand focuses on smaller, more considered batches that feel closer to the maker and more special to receive. The point is not endless variety. It is fewer offerings, made with more attention.

Each batch is approached with care in preparation, thought in presentation, and a direct relationship with the people ordering.

  • Made in small batches
  • Carefully prepared and thoughtfully packed
  • Rooted in Gujarati flavour memory
  • Limited quantities rather than continuous stock
  • Direct inquiry-based ordering
  • Suitable for personal and gifting orders
