About Keri Crafts

A brand rooted in craft.

Keri Crafts is built around thoughtful Gujarati offerings that feel rooted, considered, and personal rather than mass made.

The brand draws from Gujarati flavour memory and a way of making that values care, patience, and detail. Instead of endless variety or continuous stock, Keri Crafts focuses on smaller batches that feel more intentional, more special, and closer to the maker.

Everything about the brand is designed to feel warm, direct, and thoughtfully presented — from the offerings themselves to the way they are shared and ordered.

Signature Offerings

A closer look at what Keri Crafts offers.

Our offerings are made in limited quantities and may vary by batch and season. We are currently open for pre-orders for Batch KC01. Message us to know what is available in this batch.

Keri Chundo

Sweet-spiced grated keri, slow-made to a rich and glossy finish with a familiar Gujarati warmth.

  • Taste profile: Sweet, gently spiced, and rounded with a lingering warmth.
  • Best enjoyed with: Thepla, paratha, khakhra, or even a simple spoonful on the side.

Keri Murabbo

Soft keri pieces prepared in a gentler, sweeter profile with a thoughtful festive character.

  • Taste profile: Soft, mellow, and sweet with a delicate spiced depth.
  • Best enjoyed with: Rotis, festive meals, or as a sweeter accompaniment at the table.

Katki Keri

Chunk-cut keri gently cooked into a sweet, spiced preparation with a rich bite.

  • Taste profile: Textured, sweet-spiced, and fuller in bite and character.
  • Best enjoyed with: Thepla, phulka, or alongside everyday Gujarati meals.

God Keri

Chunk-cut keri tossed in a robust masala, delivering texture, heat, and sharpness.

  • Taste profile: Bold, masaledar, and lively with a sharper finish.
  • Best enjoyed with: Dal-rice, paratha, curd rice, or comfort meals that need a punch.

Vaghariyu

A bold keri preparation with a distinctive vaghar-led character and a deeply rooted flavour profile.

  • Taste profile: Tempered, savoury, and spice-forward with deep Gujarati character.
  • Best enjoyed with: Thepla, rotli, khichdi, or traditional home-style meals.

Amba Hardar

Keri pickle with haldi-led warmth, bringing earthy depth and traditional character.

  • Taste profile: Earthy, warm, and grounded with haldi-led depth.
  • Best enjoyed with: Rotli-shaak, dal-chawal, curd rice, or simple home meals.

Khatta Athanu

A sharper, tangier keri offering with a lively flavour profile and a more pronounced edge.

  • Taste profile: Tangy, punchy, and bright with a lively finish.
  • Best enjoyed with: Thepla, paratha, khichdi, or whenever a meal needs an instant lift.
